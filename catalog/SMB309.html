    Small and medium businesses (SMBs) can become more productive and efficient by practicing platform engineering, a discipline that focuses on building and maintaining the internal developer platform and tools. This chalk talk discusses what platform engineering is and how to build a platform in AWS. Speakers will dispel some of the myths and explore how platform engineering can be applied to reduce cognitive load on developers, increase their productivity, and ensure that the organization’s development processes are secure, scalable, and aligned with best practices.
