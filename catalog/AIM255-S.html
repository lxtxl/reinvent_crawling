    Generative AI has transformative potential in accelerating life sciences research and development activities. AI-driven molecule design, protein structure prediction, optimization of experimental protocols, and streamlining regulatory documentation submissions are all areas where gen AI can bring new value. In this industry panel, executives discuss the impact gen AI can have on drug discovery, protein engineering, genomic analysis, and regulatory approvals. The panel examines real-life examples that demonstrate time and cost savings in R&D and discusses future considerations in personalized medicine, synthetic biology, and regulatory intelligence that offer insights into gen AI's potential to reinvent the life sciences landscape.
