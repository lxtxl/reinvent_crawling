    In this session, hear how Gousto, a British meal kit retailer, built a scalable Industrial Data Fabric (IDF) to gain real-time and historical operations visibility. Gousto relies on equipment vendors to provide much of their data, which can add complexity, cost, and time delays. To resolve these challenges, AWS and an AWS Partner helped build a modern IDF using AWS IoT SiteWise, AWS IoT Core, and Amazon Monitron. Hear about the business benefits of their unified namespace architecture and how the customer uses advanced analytics, ML, and generative AI on top of their industrial data foundation.
