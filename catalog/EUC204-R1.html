    Self-managing virtual desktop infrastructure (VDI) on premises is expensive and limits IT agility. In addition, concern over potential VDI software price increases is causing organizations to accelerate plans to migrate to the cloud. This chalk talk explores considerations for migrating an on-premises VDI solution to the highly scalable and reliable AWS Cloud. Learn how to leverage Amazon WorkSpaces Core using your existing VDI software from Citrix, Omnissa, Workspot, and Leostream to reduce costs and improve your ability to quickly scale with changing business requirements. Also learn how to build a VDI migration execution plan that accelerates time to value.
