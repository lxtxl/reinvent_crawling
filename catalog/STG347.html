In this chalk talk, learn best practices for securing your data client-side, server-side, and in transit with Amazon S3 encryption options. Dive into managing your organization’s data encryption preferences using AWS Identity and Access Management (IAM) policies and Amazon S3 bucket policies. In addition, learn how to audit your organization’s encryption profile across Amazon S3.
