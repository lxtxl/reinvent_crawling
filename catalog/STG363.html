With 11 nines of data durability, S3 provides a strong foundation for data protection that you can augment with a robust set of data protection features that allow you to safeguard your data with confidence. In this chalk talk, learn how you can ensure your data is protected and recoverable from accidents, natural disasters, and malicious activity with comprehensive data protection measures.
