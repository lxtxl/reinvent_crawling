    Building modern applications increasingly requires that you complement your data with external raw data from your peers to provide more value to your end users. Access to this raw data isn’t always available due mainly to security reasons. This session demonstrates how AWS Clean Rooms helps you and your peers collaborate on your collective datasets without sharing or copying underlying data. Learn how to build an application that lets your technical community find events that align to topics they want to hear about and, as a result, make new like-minded friends.
