    AWS offers a range of storage services designed for different use cases, including migrating applications, backing up data, integrating cloud storage with on-premises applications, building data lakes, and developing cloud-native applications. With multiple options available, it's crucial to carefully evaluate your needs to avoid service mismatches and unnecessary complexity. Our aim is to provide guidance in selecting the right AWS storage service to align with workloads and application requirements, providing the flexibility to scale as needed. This chalk talk gives participants a clear understanding of which AWS storage service to use for various scenarios and situations.
