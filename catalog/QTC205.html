    The Pawsey Supercomputing Centre in Perth, Australia, is home to the largest supercomputer in the Southern Hemisphere and is spearheading the integration of quantum capabilities with traditional HPC workloads. In this chalk talk, see their architecture for running a national hub for quantum computing leveraging Amazon Braket and HPC resources. We demonstrate how to configure Braket to manage users, projects, budgets, and different devices to support a national research and education effort, all in one place.
