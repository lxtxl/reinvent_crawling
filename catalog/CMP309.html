    How can you improve the accuracy of generative AI responses—by leveraging Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG), fine-tuning techniques, or using a combination of both? This hand-on workshop explores two RAG approaches. First, learn how to implement RAG using Amazon Bedrock Knowledge Bases with quick and easy setup. Then, take a deep dive into Amazon SageMaker to fine-tune a model on AWS Trainium, deploy it on AWS Inferentia, and use Amazon OpenSearch Service to build out a RAG workflow. Learn when to employ each method based on your specific use case, domain-specific data, and tenancy requirements. You must bring your laptop to participate.
