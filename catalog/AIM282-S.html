    Business and technical leaders acknowledge generative AI’s potential to transform their industry. However, two out of three executives are dissatisfied with their organization’s progress and their inability to close the gap between ambition and implementation. In this session, Coursera CTO Mustafa Furniturewala discusses how leaders can introduce gen AI to their teams, as well as the tools, training, and operational guidance needed to identify high-impact use cases. Get practical advice on how you can accelerate productivity and innovation to deliver ROI on technology investments, learn how gen AI implementation differs operationally from cloud transformation, and hear best practices for ensuring equal opportunities for diverse workforces.
