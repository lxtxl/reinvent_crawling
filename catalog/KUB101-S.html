    Ready to take back control of your observability data? As organizations adopt Kubernetes, telemetry volumes explode. If you build and run applications on Amazon EKS, you’re likely creating higher cardinality metrics and monitoring requests traversing thousands of services. All of this makes it hard to distill the signal from the noise and keep observability within budget constraints. In this lightning talk, learn best practices for regaining control of your telemetry. Discover how to analyze telemetry usage, reduce noise within your observability platform, and enforce chargeback across your organization. This presentation is brought to you by Chronosphere, an AWS Partner.
