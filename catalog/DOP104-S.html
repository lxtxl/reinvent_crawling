    Drata faced a challenge when starting to build their product: Integrating with their customers' various HRIS and ticketing solutions was critical to streamline the compliance process. However, building and managing each one was a massive, resource-draining task that would take months. Hear directly from the CTO and cofounder of Drata on how they solved this problem with Merge. Learn how Drata saved engineering resources and accelerated deal closures with Merge’s Unified API to easily add HRIS, ticketing, and SCIM directory integrations.
