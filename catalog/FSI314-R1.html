    In this chalk talk, explore the effects of service disruptions on financial services applications and how you can address them. Learn how to assess the resiliency of your own architectures and develop strategies to strengthen your response and recovery capabilities. Review example architectures in detail and replay past service events to analyze system reliability and identify potential vulnerabilities. Leave this talk equipped with the knowledge and tools necessary to ensure your architectures can withstand disruptions, strengthen your approach to operational resilience, and minimize the impact of large-scale events.
