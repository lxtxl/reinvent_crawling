    Amazon Q helps AWS customers unlock the power of generative AI quickly for their organizations. While AWS public sector customers can also readily unlock this value, those that operate regulated workloads require additional guardrails and controls for enhanced security and manageability. In this chalk talk, learn how Amazon Q can be combined with Trusted Secure Enclaves and AWS Config to provide a way to securely deploy and manage your data for regulated workloads while enabling the transformative potential of generative AI within a secure and compliant AWS environment.
