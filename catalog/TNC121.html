    Generative AI promises immense potential, but how do you validate output quality and consistency in an era of AI-powered content creation? In this lightning talk, explore how AWS Training and Certification is innovating with generative and human intelligence while driving quality through robust scoring techniques. Learn practical methods to quickly adapt AI-generated content to customer needs while improving accuracy and trustworthiness. By examining when and why scoring matters, we provide actionable insights into creating a disciplined, repeatable process for measuring AI outputs within a range of budgets.
