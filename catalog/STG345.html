    Customers who leverage Amazon EC2 instance store for high performance local storage may encounter durability, resiliency, or data protection challenges. Mirroring EC2 instance store with EBS volumes enables faster reads and secure writes that can be protected with EBS snapshots. Join this session to learn how to build a provisioned mirrored volume as well as the cost benefits and performance advantages of this design. Discover the unique value of blending the best of EC2 instance store and EBS volumes and snapshots, allowing you to adjust performance, flexibility, and security while keeping costs in mind.
