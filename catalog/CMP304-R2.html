    Large language models (LLMs) are pretrained on vast amounts of data and perform well across a variety of general-purpose tasks and benchmarks without further specialized training. In practice, however, it is common to improve the performance of a pretrained LLM by fine-tuning the model using a smaller task-specific or domain-specific dataset. In this builders’ session, learn how to use Amazon SageMaker to fine-tune a pretrained Hugging Face LLM using AWS Trainium, and then leverage the fine-tuned model for inference. You must bring your laptop to participate.
