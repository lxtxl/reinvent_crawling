    Message brokers for point-to-point and publish-subscribe messaging are among the most widely used middleware. These systems power critical systems and integrate software products around the globe. Join this chalk talk for an interactive discussion on how AWS customers are migrating from on-premises or self-managed RabbitMQ, IBM MQ, ActiveMQ, and others to Amazon MQ. Learn best practices for building durable, scalable, global systems without managing message brokers on your own.
