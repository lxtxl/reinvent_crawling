    In this chalk talk, you learn how to build a secure global operational technology and information technology (OT/IT) network on AWS, connecting disparate and remote assets like solar farms or well sites to the cloud via OPC Unified Architecture servers, supervisory control and data acquisition systems, and programmable logic controllers. The architecture promotes a 100% private OT/IT network, segregates OT/IT traffic in transit and at rest, and implements the Purdue model on AWS.
