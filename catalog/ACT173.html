    Indigenous communities are among the most unserved and underserved populations for broadband deployment and computer science (CS) education, with Indigenous youth facing significant barriers in accessing CS instruction, creating a concerning educational disparity. Join leaders from Indigenous at Amazon to discuss the digital divide and the limitations that prevent Indigenous peoples from having access to technology, founder programs, and the services and products that digital services provide. Following the fireside chat, network and find community with fellow attendees during the Indigenous in tech meetup.
