    We all have workloads we’re not proud of: they’re old, often brittle, but they keep the mission running. Nonprofits have limited time and resources, so how do you even begin to upgrade or replace a legacy workload? In this builders’ session, get hands-on to move a legacy mission-based application into a modern cloud-native architecture. Modernize the routing and database layers of an existing monolithic application, and learn how to gradually shift traffic to your new stack. Leave this session knowing how to modernize your legacy workloads with confidence. You must bring your laptop to participate.
