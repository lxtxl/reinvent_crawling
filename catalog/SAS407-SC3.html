    Multi-tenancy adds a set of new architecture, design, and implementation considerations to the generative AI domain. This session digs into a working multi-tenant generative AI solution, looking closely at how tenancy impacts the implementation of data partitioning, tenant isolation, tenant onboarding, noisy neighbor, tiering, and so on. Learn how to support generative AI experiences that are differentiated for individual tenants. The goal is to provide an in-depth view of all the moving parts of a complete SaaS experience that uses Amazon Bedrock, knowledge bases, vector databases, and more.
