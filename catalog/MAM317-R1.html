    In the cloud-centered era, monolithic databases are becoming increasingly challenging to scale and maintain as applications grow in complexity. The traditional monolithic approach can hinder performance, resilience, and agility, acting as a bottleneck for modern applications. Join this chalk talk to explore strategies for decomposing a monolithic database into a distributed, scalable architecture leveraging AWS services. Discuss techniques for breaking down data domains, implementing event-driven architectures, and utilizing managed services like Amazon Aurora, Amazon DynamoDB, and Amazon ElastiCache. Also, learn best practices for data interfacing, database splitting, aggregation exposition, and data synchronization to keep the application responsive while increasing the modularity and decoupling of the database.
