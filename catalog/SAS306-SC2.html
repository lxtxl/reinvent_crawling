    SaaS builders must balance a range of competing needs when selecting a multi-tenant storage strategy. How you'll scale, how you'll prevent noisy neighbor, how you'll isolate tenant data, how you'll support remote data, how you'll shard workloads—these are among the many factors you must consider. This session examines the different strategies and patterns that are applied when designing a scalable multi-tenant data architecture that can evolve as your business evolves. This goes beyond data partitioning, looking into the details of how you address challenging multi-tenant storage models and balance cost, operational, and performance efficiency.
