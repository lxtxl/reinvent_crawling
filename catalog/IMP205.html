    Nonprofits operate under budgetary and resource constraints that significantly hamper their ability to fulfill their mission. One such hurdle is aligning IT infrastructure with strategic goals and operational needs. IT transformation can deliver higher economic value, agility, and rapid innovation. In this chalk talk, business leaders and technologists learn modernization pathways, tools, and technologies to build a business case for prioritizing IT modernization. Presenters whiteboard use cases to move to cloud native architectures, containers, managed databases, modern DevOps, and open source. Attendees leave having learned repeatable patterns and practices for rapid modernization to lead to positive mission outcomes.
