    AI-based chatbots are transforming how we use and build applications. As a tech innovator, the Washington Post built an LLM-powered chatbot trained on custom data including documentation, internal processes, and runbooks using Amazon Bedrock. But LLMs can also come with challenges, such as identifying hallucinations at scale, protecting against abuse from prompt injection, and ensuring against inadvertent disclosure of data. In this session, Joe Croney, CTO of the Washington Post’s Arc XP Business, shares how they built the chatbot and how they use LLM observability to ensure it not only meets customer needs but is used ethically and responsibly.
