    Explore the spectrum of connectivity options to securely access your AWS hosted applications. Compare traditional VPN approaches, including AWS Site-to-Site VPN and AWS Client VPN, with VPN-less, Zero Trust-based solutions like AWS Verified Access. Understand the trade-offs, capabilities, and ideal use cases for each approach. By the end of this chalk talk, leave equipped to choose the connectivity solution that best meets your organization’s security, performance, and operational requirements. Walk away with the insights to enable seamless, protected access to your AWS resources, whether leveraging VPN or embracing VPN-less architectures.
