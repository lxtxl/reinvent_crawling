    As the volume and value of cloud data surges, defending it is an ever-increasing challenge. In 2022, 94% of cloud tenants were targeted every month, and 62% were successfully compromised. A focus on preventing incidents is no longer sufficient—data must be defensible, even after a compromise. This session explores how to expand your cybersecurity strategy, helping you control risk, recover quickly and safely in the event of an incident, and ensure your data and business stand resilient against cyberattacks.
