    In the rapidly evolving open source landscape, navigating complex connectivity patterns while maintaining non-functional requirements can be challenging. This is one of the reasons why the industry has embraced service mesh technologies to centralize and minimize those complexities. However, with numerous service mesh options and projects like Kubernetes Gateway API and Ambient Mesh emerging, choosing the right solution can be overwhelming. In this chalk talk, learn how to navigate this landscape and select the most suitable solution to achieve your specific business objectives.
