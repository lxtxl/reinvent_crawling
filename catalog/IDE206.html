    Global manufacturer Jabil faced challenges with frontline talent management, including high attrition, inefficient hiring, and lack of upskilling. To address, Jabil partnered with AWS to develop an end-to-end talent transformation. This included leveraging AWS services to build a robust data and technology infrastructure, redesigning processes, and driving cultural transformation focused on inclusion, diversity, and equity (ID&E). With a holistic approach to ID&E, Jabil aimed to create a more inclusive work environment to foster greater employee engagement, productivity, and retention. In this lightning talk, Jabil and AWS discuss building inclusive solutions at scale and share learnings from their talent transformation journey.
