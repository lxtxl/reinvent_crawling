    Platform engineering is the backbone of engineering teams, but its true potential is unlocked with a product management mindset. In this lightning talk, hear the CTO and cofounder of Cortex.io explain how platform engineers can enhance their impact by treating their internal developer portal as a product. By understanding internal customers’ needs, prioritizing features, and ensuring a seamless developer experience, platform engineers can substantially increase their team’s productivity. Join us to discover how adopting this mindset can transform your platform engineering efforts into a high-impact, developer-centric initiative that empowers your teams and drives organizational success.
