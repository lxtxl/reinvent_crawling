    Over the past few years, federal agencies and data providers have increasingly required more stringent security and privacy controls for their data. In this session, learn how Harvard University’s Research Computing organization pivoted to create a dedicated team and collaborated with AWS to create a regulated data environment. Explore how they built basic core services (FISMA-compliant landing zone accelerator and advanced security features) in just 12 weeks and also built research environments using Open OnDemand, AWS ParallelCluster, and Research Engineering Studio on AWS. Learn valuable strategies and technical guidance from Harvard Research Computing to meet research data compliance by leveraging the cloud.
